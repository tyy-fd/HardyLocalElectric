What We Offer

Our Electrical Services

Fully certified domestic and commercial electrical work across Bromley and surrounding areas. No job too big or too small.

01

Part & Whole Re-Wires

Whether your property needs a complete rewire or just a partial update to meet modern safety standards, our qualified team delivers safe, tidy, and fully certified work.

02

24/7 Emergency Callout – Blackouts

Power failure? Tripped fuse board? We offer round-the-clock emergency response. Call us any time and we'll be with you as soon as possible.

03

Fault Finding & Diagnostics

Intermittent faults and unexplained electrical issues can be dangerous. We use professional diagnostic tools to identify and fix problems quickly and safely.

04

New Installs

New build, extension, or renovation? We handle all new electrical installations from consumer units to full first and second fix wiring.

05

EICR & Remedials

Electrical Installation Condition Reports (EICR) are required for landlords and recommended for homeowners. We test, certify, and carry out any remedial work needed.

06

Lighting Installations

Recessed downlights, pendants, track lighting, under-cabinet strips, outdoor and garden lighting — we fit it all to a professional finish.

07

Power Installations

Extra sockets, fused spurs, consumer unit upgrades, EV charger prep — all power installation work carried out to 18th Edition wiring regulations.

08

Fire Alarm Systems

Design, installation and commissioning of fire alarm systems for domestic and commercial properties. We ensure full compliance with current regulations.

09

Security Camera Installations

CCTV and IP camera systems for homes and businesses. Professionally installed and positioned for maximum coverage and peace of mind.

10

Quotes & Surveying

Free no-obligation quotes and full electrical surveys. We'll assess your property and provide clear, honest pricing before any work begins.

11

LED Lighting Upgrades

Switch to modern LED lighting and reduce your energy bills significantly. We supply quality fittings and handle the full installation.

12

Energy-Saving Installations

Smart switches, motion sensors, dimmer controls and energy monitoring — we help you make your property more efficient and reduce running costs.
